Genetic testing

Genetic testing will tell you if your child has a change, or a mutation, in certain genes called PEX genes that are attributed to PBD-ZSD. A genetic test is available to qualifying patients at no cost that can help diagnose PBD-ZSD.

If the genetic test comes back positive for PBD-ZSD, an additional test called an atypical bile acid test is performed to determine if the liver is impacted by the condition.

Your doctor can access this genetic testing program by visiting preventiongenetics.com. Talk to your doctor about genetic testing for PBD-ZSD today.
